Our Story

Built for how we play here.

FORZA started with a question one of our founders couldn't stop asking. After fifteen years on Indian pitches and four years analysing pro footballers frame-by-frame for elite clubs, why was no one solving the foot-shoe interface for the way we actually play here? Indian heat. Indian turf. Indian feet. The first homegrown grip socks built for the player who treats every session like it matters.

From pitch to product.

FORZA wasn't built in a boardroom. It was built between Saturday morning sessions in Bengaluru, Sunday tournaments in Mumbai, and corporate league nights in Pune. Two founders. One question. What would the trusted pair look like if it was built by someone who actually plays?

Seven prototypes. Hundreds of player conversations. Years of taping fingers before every match to keep blisters off, pouring water into our socks when the heat got too much, never quite trusting what was inside the boot. Stability under the foot was something we never thought about. Until we did. The product is the answer.

What Drives Us

Our principles.

Equipment, not apparel.

We make gear that solves a real problem on the pitch. Not lifestyle. Not fashion. Engineered grip socks that hold through ninety minutes plus extra time, in the heat we actually play in.

Built here, for here.

Indian climate, Indian turf, Indian feet, Indian play. The wedge no imported brand can claim. Our climate is a product spec, not a marketing line.

One pair at a time.

We'd rather get one thing right than launch ten things you'll forget about. Phase one is socks. The rest comes when it's ready. Wear a pair. You'll know.
